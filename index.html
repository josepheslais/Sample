When you are feeling overwhelmed or out of control it can help to talk about your feelings. However, venting without seeing a path to change can feel like going around in circles with no way out. The Eslais Clinic can help you with overwhelming feelings of anxiety, depression, hopelessness, and cycles of self-harm. At the Eslais Clinic we will assist you in achieving access to living the life you want to lead. We provide specialist Training and Supervision to practitioners working in the area of mental health in Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT) informed treatments. The Eslais Clinic team is made up of specialist psychiatrists, clinical psychologists, occupational therapists and trained nurses. We have on-site referral links to experienced psychologists, specialising in educational assessment, child and adolescent therapy, adult psychotherapy, couple therapy, family therapy, and divorce mediation.
